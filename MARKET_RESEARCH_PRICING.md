# Market Research - Vehicle Inspection Industry Pricing

## Current Market Rates (2024-2025)

### What Inspectors Charge Customers:

**Mobile Inspectors:**
- Basic inspection: $100-$150 (30-45 min)
- Comprehensive: $150-$250 (60-90 min)
- Premium/specialty: $250-$400 (luxury, exotic, RVs)
- Pre-purchase inspection: $200-$350 (most common)

**Geographic Variance:**
- Rural/small cities: $100-$150
- Suburban: $150-$200
- Major metros (LA, NYC, SF): $200-$350
- **Sweet spot nationwide: $175-$200**

### Competitor Pricing Models:

**Lemon Squad (Marketplace Model):**
- Customer pays: $149-$199
- Inspector receives: ~$100-$130 (30-35% platform fee)
- Lemon Squad keeps: $49-$69 per inspection
- **Revenue share: 33-35%**

**YourMechanic (Marketplace Model):**
- Customer pays: $100-$200
- Mechanic receives: ~65-70%
- Platform keeps: 30-35%
- **Revenue share: 30-35%**

**Pre-Purchase Car Inspections (Independent):**
- Inspector keeps 100%
- Average price: $200
- No platform fee
- Must do own marketing

### Software Competitors:

**AutoLeap (Shop Management):**
- $199/month
- No revenue share
- For repair shops, not mobile inspectors

**Shopmonkey:**
- $165/month per user
- No revenue share
- Shop management focus

**Mobile Tech RX:**
- $99/month basic
- $199/month pro
- Mobile-first but basic features
- **No AI, no marketing automation**

**RepairPal Pro:**
- Custom pricing (~$300-500/month)
- Quote generation focus
- Not inspection-specific

### Key Market Insights:

1. **No one offers marketing automation** - massive gap
2. **Marketplace models charge 30-35%** - inspectors accept this for leads
3. **SaaS-only tools are $99-$199/month** - limited features
4. **Average inspection price is rising** - $175-$200 now standard
5. **High-volume inspectors** (30+/month) make $60-80K/year
6. **Part-time inspectors** (5-10/month) make $10-15K/year supplemental
